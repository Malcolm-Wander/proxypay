Thank you for installing the Mobile Money Helm chart.

To deploy:
  helm install proxypay ./k8s/helm --namespace proxypay --create-namespace

To verify the installation:
  kubectl get deployments,services,pods,hpa -n proxypay

Access the backend service inside the cluster via:
  kubectl get svc {{ include "proxypay.fullname" . }} -n proxypay

If Redis is enabled, it is available as:
  {{ include "proxypay.fullname" . }}-redis

To uninstall:
  helm uninstall proxypay -n proxypay
